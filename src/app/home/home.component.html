Earth is the third planet in our system. For all people it is a unique place to live, but for the universe it is only one small point. As much as 71% of our planet's surface are oceans and seas, which are subject to high tides and outflows caused by our natural satellite - the Moon, and the Sun. It has flattened poles, which is why it is not a sphere and so-called geoid. The magnetic field produced by the Earth protects us through lethal solar and cosmic rays.
